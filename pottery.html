Pottery

At Tellie Studios, we believe that clay tells stories—of creativity, connection, and care. Each piece in our collection is thoughtfully handcrafted, celebrating the textures of nature and the spirit of play. Whether you’re exploring our whimsical bunnies, galaxy-inspired mugs, or earthy mushrooms, you’ll find artistry and warmth in every curve.

Each piece is individually priced. If a design has multiple variations, your chosen option will appear in your cart—just leave your selection in the cart message section. For custom sizes or color modifications, reach out through our Contact Page.
