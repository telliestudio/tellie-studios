About TellieStudios

Bringing art to life, one brushstroke at a time.

TellieStudios was founded with the belief that art is more than just an aesthetic—it's a journey, an expression, and a way to connect. Our mission is to inspire creativity, provide quality art resources, and create spaces where artists of all levels can feel supported in their craft.

Creativity

We foster an environment where imagination thrives. Whether through hands-on workshops or curated art kits, we encourage self-expression.

Community

Art is a universal language, and we build connections through shared passion, thoughtful collaborations, and meaningful conversations.

Quality

We believe in delivering the best—from materials to workshops—ensuring every artist has the tools needed to create with confidence.
